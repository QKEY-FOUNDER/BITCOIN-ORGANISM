# Market to Music Translator

This file defines how Bitcoin market data becomes sound.

The market does not create random music.
It creates biological vibration.

---

## Input Data

For each candle:

- Open
- High
- Low
- Close
- Volume
- BTC Dominance
- Time
- Evolutionary Phase

---

## Candle → Sound Mapping

### Candle Body (Open → Close)
Controls melodic movement.

Large green body → strong upward interval  
Large red body → strong downward interval  
Small body → subtle motion  

---

### Wick Length (High – Low)
Controls articulation.

Long wicks → sharp attacks, shimmering transients  
Short wicks → smooth legato  

---

### Candle Size (High – Low)
Controls dynamics.

Large range → loud  
Small range → quiet  

---

### Volume
Controls density.

Low volume → few notes  
High volume → dense texture  
Explosive volume → crescendos  

---

### BTC Dominance
Controls tonal gravity.

High dominance → strong D minor center  
Low dominance → drifting harmonies  

---

### Time of Day (Global Sessions)

Asia → soft pads  
Europe → rhythmic clarity  
America → drums, climax  

---

### Evolutionary Phase
Controls style.

Genesis → minimal  
Bull → epic  
Winter → dark  
Rebirth → bright  

---

## Why this exists

This is how charts become songs.

The Bitcoin-Organism does not “represent” the market.
It **is** the market, vibrating.
